> **Learning note — Product Roadmap**
> - **Why**: Communicates strategy through time — answers "what are we building, in what order, and why?"
> - **Who uses it**: PM drives alignment; Engineering allocates capacity; Sales manages customer expectations; Leadership evaluates strategy
> - **Key decisions**: What's in Now, Next, Later — and what's explicitly *Not Doing* and why
> - **Next step**: Approved roadmap feeds into release planning and cycle planning
