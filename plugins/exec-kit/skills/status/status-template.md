> **Learning note — Status Report**
> - **Why**: Proactively surfaces project health, risks, and decisions needed — without requiring stakeholders to attend every meeting
> - **Who uses it**: PM owns it; stakeholders evaluate health and provide input; leadership decides on escalation or resource changes
> - **Key decisions**: What is the honest RAG status across schedule, scope, quality, and team? What decisions are needed from stakeholders this period?
> - **Next step**: Distributed at each cycle close; open decisions tracked until resolved; risks escalated as needed
